## 🧬 Architectural Journey

My skill acquisition follows a modular, high-retention learning model.

### 🏁 **Phase 1: Foundations**
> **Core CS & C++** $\rightarrow$ **DSA & Algorithms**
> *Focus: Algorithmic Efficiency & Design Patterns.*

### 🌐 **Phase 2: Modern Web**
> **Advanced ES6+ & RWD** $\rightarrow$ **TypeScript & Architecture**
> *Focus: Reactive UI & Type-Safe Scalability.*

### ⚙️ **Phase 3: Backend Systems**
> **Node.js & API Design** $\rightarrow$ **SQL Optimization**
> *Focus: Scalable Server Architecture & Data Integrity.*

### 🏆 **Phase 4: Specialization**
> **PWA & Modern Standards** $\rightarrow$ **Final Polish & Mastery**
> *Focus: Enterprise-Grade Deployment & Standards.*
